History

Seethawaka Kingdom is brightened up with the new arrival of the prestigious Lavenro Garden Hotel. This is the ideal location for a glamorous outlook and a place to create everlasting memories in life.

When the Portugese arrived in Sri Lanka in 1505, they found an island comprising several kingdoms. At the time, as the Kandyan Kingdom, there was the aspiring Seethawaka Kingdom as well. The Portugese soon learnt that Seethawaka held the balance of power and posed the greatest threat to their expansionist intension.

This town has a rich and proud history in the annals of its military exploits against the Portugese invaders as it was the capital of Seethawaka Kingdom from 1521 to 1593. Seethawaka was ruled by King Mayadunne and later his son Warrior Rajasinghe the First during this period.

The splendid Lavenro Garden Hotel is situated in this historic kingdom. In a serene surrounding located along the Colombo-Rathnapura Main Road captures the heart of the Seethawaka Kingdom with plenty of eye-catching locations to make your event of any nature unforgettable.
